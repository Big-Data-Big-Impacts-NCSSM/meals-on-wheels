Meals on Wheels 2021 Report

Health and Wellbeing

Over 90% of clients say Meals on Wheels Durham improves overall health.

88% of clients report eating healthier because of MOWD meals.
Over 75% of clients said that MOWD helped maintain or improve health conditions such as diabetes, heart disease, high blood pressure, etc.

Affordability

2/3 of clients say they wouldn’t be able to afford nutritious food if not for Meals on Wheels Durham.

1/2 of clients say receiving meals from Meals on Wheels Durham is one of the reasons they can continue living in their own home.
Under half of clients with pets are unable to afford pet food if not for MOWD’s Pet Program.
