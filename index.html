Affordability

Could you afford to buy enough nutritious food if you were not receiving food from Meals on Wheels Durham?
Two-thirds of participants wouldn't be able to afford nutritious food without Meals on Wheels Durham

Is receiving meals from Meals on Wheels Durham one of the reasons you can continue living in your own home?
Meals on Wheels saves your family money: you don't need to pay for nursery homes. MOWD helps seniors continue to live in their own homes.

Variety and Impact

Do you think that the menu variety that is provided is satisfactory?
4 in 5 participants are happy about the variety of menus that Meals on Wheels Durham provides.

Could you always safely cook by yourself without Meals on Wheels Durham?
About 9 out of 10 participants could not safely prepare meals all the time without Meals on Wheels Durham.

Volunteer and Program Satisfaction

Are volunteer drivers and callers ALWAYS helpful?
About 95% of participants reported that volunteer drivers are helpful all the time.

Would you recommend Meals on Wheels Durham to somebody else?
99.61% of members would recommend Meals on Wheels Durham to somebody else.

Pets

Could you afford food for your pet without Meals on Wheels?

Menu Breakdown

Lean Protein
Each person is provided one serving of a lean protein. Choices supplied are beef, chicken, and pork.
Fruit and Veggies
People get two servings of either a fruit or a vegetable and can choose which.
Milk
Eight ounces of milk are given to each person.
Complex Carbohydrates
Two servings of complex carbohydrates are supplied. Usually 1 bread and 1 starch are given.
